CJ Connoy

Founder & Director, One Day Games. 32 years across Sony PlayStation, Treyarch, and Sony Interactive Entertainment.

CJ spent over a decade at Treyarch on the Call of Duty: Black Ops franchise (Black Ops 1 through 4).

He was Founding Senior Producer at Deviation Games, a Sony-backed studio building original AAA IP from zero, and Founding Senior Lead, Design at Sony first-party Dark Outlaw Games — where he started the co-op prototype that became the foundation of the studio's main AAA IP.

One Day Games is what CJ is doing now: a solo studio building faithful digital companions to physical games. The work is a translation problem more than a porting one — taking a finished physical game and authoring its digital companion without losing what made the original feel like itself.
